A Trend Micro account is a secure online profile that stores your subscription information, registered devices, and personal settings. Instead of managing product keys separately, your account keeps everything in one place, making it easier to install, renew, or transfer your Trend Micro products.

Depending on the products you own, your account may allow you to:

View active subscriptions.
Download Trend Micro software.
Activate licenses on new devices.
Manage protected computers and mobile devices.
Renew or upgrade subscriptions.
Access Password Manager services.
Update billing or account information.
Contact customer support.

Having a Trend Micro account simplifies device management while helping you keep your digital security up to date.

How to Log In to Your Trend Micro Account

Logging in is a simple process that takes only a few minutes.

Follow these steps:

Open your preferred web browser.
Visit the official Trend Micro account sign-in page.
Enter the email address associated with your account.
Type your password carefully.
Select Sign In or Log In.
Complete any requested verification step if prompted.
After successful authentication, your account dashboard will open.

From the dashboard, you can access your subscriptions, registered devices, downloads, and account settings.

Recovering a Forgotten Password

If you forget your password, resetting it is quick and easy.

Follow these steps:

Open the Trend Micro login page.
Select Forgot Password.
Enter your registered email address.
Submit the password reset request.
Check your inbox for the reset email.
Click the password reset link.
Create a new secure password.
Return to the login page and sign in.

If you do not receive the reset email, check your Spam or Junk folder before requesting another reset.

Common Login Problems

Although most users sign in without difficulty, several common issues may prevent access.

Incorrect Email Address

Ensure you are using the same email address that was used when registering your Trend Micro account.

Incorrect Password

Passwords are case-sensitive. Carefully verify capitalization and avoid extra spaces.

Email Verification Pending

Some new accounts require email verification before the first login. Complete the verification process before signing in.

Browser Problems

If the login page is not functioning correctly, clear your browser's cache and cookies or try another supported browser.

Internet Connection Issues

A weak or unstable internet connection can interrupt the authentication process. Verify your connection before attempting to log in again.

Expired Subscription Confusion

Even if your subscription has expired, you should generally still be able to sign in to your account. Subscription status does not usually prevent account access.
